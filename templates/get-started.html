What is BunkBuddy?

BunkBuddy is the ultimate student companion platform designed to bridge the gap between academic responsibilities and campus life. We believe engineering or college life shouldn't just be about stress. BunkBuddy uses automated predictive logic to calculate strict attendance thresholds, allowing you to manage your presence efficiently without compromising your semester criteria.

Core Ecosystem Features

Bunk Calculator

An intelligent script tracking your semester timeline to determine exactly how many consecutive classes you can skip or must attend to preserve your absolute target.

CGPA Predictor

Leverages robust Machine Learning architectures to forecast your definitive final grade-point averages based on internal evaluations and custom trends.

Smart Alerts

Generates instantaneous, color-coded threshold metrics to safely warn you before your metrics approach critical academic danger zones.
